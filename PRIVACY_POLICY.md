1. Overview

Dice & Dots is an app that allows users to create printable dice mosaic designs from images. The App works locally on your device. You can choose an image from your photo library, take a photo with your camera, or import an image from your files. The App then processes the image on your device and can generate a printable PDF.

We do not operate user accounts, we do not run our own backend server, and we do not intentionally collect or store your personal data on our servers.

2. Information Processed by the App

The App may process the following information locally on your device:

- images selected by you from your photo library, camera, or files;
- generated mosaic previews;
- generated PDF files;
- app settings such as language, units, mosaic size, dice settings, and similar preferences;
- information about in-app purchase status needed to unlock purchased features.

Images and generated files are processed locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.

3. Camera, Photos and Files Permissions

The App may request access to:

- Camera: to allow you to take a photo and use it to create a mosaic.
- Photo Library: to allow you to select an image from your device.
- Files: to allow you to import an image file.

These permissions are used only to provide the App's core functionality. You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases, such as a single mosaic export or a lifetime unlock.

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details or full billing information. Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple's own privacy policy and App Store terms.

The App may use purchase status information to unlock paid features or restore purchases.

5. Sharing and Printing

The App may allow you to print or share a generated PDF using system features available on your device. Sharing only happens when you choose to use the share or print option.

When you share a PDF with another app or service, the handling of that file is governed by the privacy policy of the selected app or service.

6. Analytics, Advertising and Tracking

The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

The App does not use third-party analytics services.

The App does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising or advertising measurement purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13, or under the age required by applicable law in your country.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us at szaroczarnytrzmiel@gmail.com.

8. Legal Basis for Processing under GDPR

If the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, the legal bases for processing are:

- performance of a contract, when the App processes information necessary to provide its features or paid functionality;
- your consent, when you grant device permissions such as camera or photo library access;
- legitimate interests, where necessary to maintain the App's functionality and security.

Because the App does not use our own backend account system and does not upload your images to our servers, most processing happens locally on your device.

9. Data Retention

Images, generated mosaics, PDFs and settings are stored locally on your device only as needed for the App's functionality.

You can delete local data by deleting generated files, clearing app data where supported by your device, or uninstalling the App.

Purchase records may be retained by Apple according to Apple's own legal and accounting obligations.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your images with third parties from our own servers, because we do not upload them to our servers.

Information may be processed by Apple when you download the App, make an in-app purchase, restore purchases, or use App Store services. Apple processes such information according to its own privacy policy.

11. International Transfers

We do not transfer your images or generated mosaics to our own servers.

Apple may process App Store and purchase-related information in countries outside your country of residence, according to Apple's own privacy and legal terms.
